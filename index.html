About

I am a fouth-year Ph.D. student in Vision and System Design Lab , HKUST, supervised by Prof. Kwang-Ting Tim CHENG .

Currently, I am a visiting scholar in the CyLab, ECE Department, CMU, advised by Prof. Marios Savvides.

I was a research intern in Face++ and Tencent AI lab , mentored by Xiangyu Zhang , Jian Sun (Face++) and Wenhan Luo , Baoyuan Wu , Wei Liu (Tencent).

Research Interest

My research interests are mainly in computer vision, machine learning. Specifically, I am interested in compact network design, including network binarization, channel pruning and neural architecture search.
